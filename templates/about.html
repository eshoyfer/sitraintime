SI Train Time was developed by Elvin Shoyfer in July 2014 with the aim of creating a quick and simple way to look up relevant train times along the Staten Island Railway (SIR) on mobile devices.

Times are calculated based on the official MTA Staten Island Railway Timetable provided by the Metropolitan Transportation Authority (MTA). The most recent version is available here on their website.

All variations in schedule on the basis of weekday or holiday status are accounted for. Train times are calculations (not real-time) based on the official timetable and may vary due to delays and flunctuations associated with train operation.

This site is not affiliated with the Metropolitan Transportation Authority.
